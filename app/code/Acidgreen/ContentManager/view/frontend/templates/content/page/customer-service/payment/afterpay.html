How does the ability to buy now/wear now, but pay later sound? When you select Afterpay in the checkout, you’ll get what you want, right now (we’ll ship the order straight away), and you’ll pay it off over 4x fortnightly payments. Babes…there’s more good news – there’s no interest!

Sound too good to be true?

  • Use Afterpay on any order up to $1000
  • All orders shipped straight away!
  • Pay it off over 4 fortnightly payments
  • No interest… at all!* + Instant approval

Things to keep in mind.

  • You must to have a  Mastercard or Visa
  • You must be 18 years or older
  • You need to be living in Australia
  • If you miss a payment, you  will be charged additional fees (see afterpay.com.au/terms)
