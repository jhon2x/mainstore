Pick Up

Now you can beat the weekend rush and conveniently collect your Peppermayo order if you are in Sydney! We are offering pick up from our Summer Pop Up Store in Waterloo NSW for the whole of Jan!

How does it work?

Just choose the ‘Pick Up’ option at checkout if your shipping address is in an eligible location in Sydney (if you’re wanting pick up and you’re outside of Sydney or interstate, you can set  your shipping address to our warehouse address below, which will activate the Pick Up shipping option).

Once your order is ready to be picked up, you will receive an email notification. You can pick up your order anytime after receiving this confirmation during our opening hours.

How long before I can pick up my order?

Once you make your order with the ‘Pick Up’ option, please allow 1 to 3 hours Monday to Friday for our warehouse team to collect your order. If you need your order urgently, please contact us  and we will expedite the collection of your order.

If you order on the weekend, our order will be available to be picked up Monday morning.

What will I need to pick up my order?

You will need to present your order confirmation on your smart phone and your ID to our friendly staff when collecting your order.
